Overview

Purpose

This site is meant to provide a place that will allow people to bring the wonders of nature to their home via breathtaking photography! Through these photos people will be able to travel from the comfort of their living room by viewing available collections. If there is a place that is not listed, requests can be made to bring the beauty of that area to home.

Audience

It is designed so that anyone who wishes to view wonders of the world can do so at their convienence. Man, woman, and child will be able to enjoy nature even on the rainiest or most busy of days.
